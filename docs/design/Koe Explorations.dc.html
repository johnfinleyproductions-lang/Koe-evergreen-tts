listen-reading desk · 聴く読書
Hear anything instantly in a natural voice, then keep your hands and eyes free to think on paper. A floating player that pops up anywhere — fused with a warm, tactile notebook for catching ideas as you listen.
Direction
Japanese stationery — kinari paper, sumi ink, washi tape, a vermilion hanko. Calm, hand-made, player-first.
On the board
The kit, then 3 floating-player treatments, 2 listen-&-annotate reading views, and the idea canvas.
How to use it
These are atoms to mix & match — pick a player + a reading view + the canvas, and I'll build the chosen flow.
